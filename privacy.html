Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 21, 2026

Momo Uploader is a private, internal automation tool built and operated by a single developer to manage video uploads for the "Momo & Bobo - Kids Songs & Nursery Rhymes" YouTube channel. It is not distributed publicly and has no external users.

What data this app accesses

Momo Uploader connects to the developer's own Google account via Google OAuth and the YouTube Data API to:

  • Upload videos and set their title, description, tags, and category
  • Read basic channel information and video statistics
  • Read YouTube Analytics engagement data (views, watch time) for the developer's own channel

How this data is used

All accessed data is used exclusively to operate and monitor the developer's own YouTube channel. No data is sold, shared with third parties, or used for advertising purposes.

Data storage

OAuth credentials are stored locally in the developer's private environment and are never transmitted to any third party.
