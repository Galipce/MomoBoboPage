Momo Uploader

Application Name: Momo Uploader

A private video-publishing tool for the "Momo & Bobo - Kids Songs & Nursery Rhymes" YouTube channel.

What Momo Uploader does

Momo Uploader is an internal, single-user application built and operated by its developer. Its only purpose is to automatically publish new videos to the developer's own YouTube channel, "Momo & Bobo - Kids Songs & Nursery Rhymes."

Specifically, Momo Uploader connects to the developer's Google account through Google Sign-In and the YouTube Data API to:

  • Upload finished video files directly to the channel
  • Set each video's title, description, tags, and category automatically
  • Read basic channel information and view-count statistics to monitor performance

Who uses this application

Momo Uploader is not a public product. It has exactly one user — its developer — and is not installed, downloaded, or used by anyone else.
