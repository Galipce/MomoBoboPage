Momo Uploader

Momo Uploader is a private, internal automation tool used solely by its developer to publish video content to the "Momo & Bobo - Kids Songs & Nursery Rhymes" YouTube channel.

It uses the YouTube Data API to upload videos, set metadata, and read basic channel analytics for the developer's own channel. It is not a public-facing product and is not distributed to or used by any other end users.
